Share - Split

firebase realtime database
Share - Split is the easiest way to share expenses with friends and family and stop stressing about “who owes who.”
This is a web-based application. The app manages transactions in the cloud.
Share - Split is great for:
- Friends splitting restaurant bills between 2 people
(more than 2 people split bills will come later)
-The users do not know the exact expenses (guessing) so the app will calculate the final payment
Share - Split is simple to use:
Treasurer pay the bill
The creditor has a transaction code and expenses
Debtor pay the credit to the Treasurer
The borrower adds the transaction code and expenses
The back end process will calculate the final payment
